About MindCare AI

Your compassionate AI companion for mental wellness

Our Mission

MindCare AI is dedicated to making mental health support accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone deserves a safe space to express their feelings and receive compassionate guidance. Our AI-powered chatbot combines advanced emotion detection with empathetic responses to provide 24/7 support.

How It Works

Emotion Detection

Advanced NLP models analyze your messages to understand your emotional state in real-time.

Sentiment Analysis

Our system evaluates the sentiment of your messages to provide contextually appropriate responses.

Mood Tracking

Log and track your moods over time to identify patterns and triggers in your mental health journey.

Personalized Suggestions

Receive daily wellness tips and coping strategies tailored to your emotional state.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. All conversations are stored locally and securely. We do not share your data with third parties. However, this tool is for supportive purposes only and should not replace professional mental health care.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python Flask
  • AI Models: Hugging Face Transformers (DistilBERT)
  • NLP: Emotion Detection & Sentiment Analysis
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Important Disclaimer

MindCare AI is designed to provide emotional support and information but is NOT a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, severe depression, suicidal thoughts, or any serious mental health condition, please contact a mental health professional or crisis helpline immediately.
